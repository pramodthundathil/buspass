Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bus pass system

The required documents vary by pass type. Students need a valid student ID and address proof. Employees need an employee ID and address proof. Regular commuters need a government ID and address proof. All documents should be in JPG, PNG, or PDF format.

Typically, the verification process takes 24-48 hours after document submission. Once approved, you'll receive a notification, and your pass will be available for download or collection.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and mobile wallets like Paytm and PhonePe. All transactions are secured with 128-bit encryption.

Yes, you can cancel your bus pass through the cancellation module. Refunds are processed based on our policy - full refund if canceled within 7 days of issue and no usage, partial refund if canceled before the halfway mark of validity.

Renewing your pass is simple. Log in to your account, go to the "My Passes" section, and click on the "Renew" button next to your active pass. Choose the duration and proceed with payment. Your pass will be renewed with the new validity period.
